Google Ads Cost in Kerala: PPC Budget Guide

By Abin Vinod June 25, 2026 7 min read

For businesses looking for instant leads and brand visibility, Google Ads is the ultimate channel. However, one of the most common questions business owners in Kerala ask is: **"How much does Google Ads cost?"**

This guide explains how Google Ads bidding works, average Cost-Per-Click (CPC) rates in Kerala, and how much you should expect to spend on setup and management fees.

Understanding the Google Ads Pricing Model

Google Ads runs on an auction system. You only pay when someone actually clicks on your ad (known as PPC or Pay-Per-Click). The cost of a click depends on various factors:

  • Keyword Competitiveness: High-demand keywords (e.g., "best real estate in Kochi") cost more than low-competition niches.
  • Quality Score: Google rewards relevant, high-quality landing pages and ads with lower costs per click.
  • Geographic Location: Targeting specific high-density zones in Kerala (like Ernakulam) may have higher bid requirements.

Average Costs in Kerala (Industry Benchmarks)

Here are typical CPC rates for campaigns targeted within Kerala:

  • Real Estate & Construction: ₹30 - ₹90 per click.
  • Education & Training Institutes: ₹15 - ₹40 per click.
  • Healthcare & Clinics: ₹20 - ₹60 per click.
  • Local Services (Plumbers, Cleaners, etc.): ₹10 - ₹25 per click.

PPC Management & Consulting Fees

Besides the direct ad spend paid to Google, hiring an expert to design, monitor, and optimize your campaigns involves management fees. These generally follow one of these formats:

  • Fixed Monthly Fee: Ideal for small to mid-sized campaigns (ranges from ₹10,000 to ₹25,000/month).
  • Percentage of Ad Spend: Common for large-scale enterprise accounts (typically 10% to 20% of the total monthly spend).
